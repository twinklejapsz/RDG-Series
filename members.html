THE ROYAL MEMBERS

RDG is an eleven-member group famously known as the Royalties in Devil’s Suit — rich, influential, and powerful figures who rule both the streets and the shadows. Bound by loyalty and legacy, their presence commands fear and respect.
The gang is divided into two orders. The RDG Kings stand as the founders and pillars of the empire, while the RDG Princes serve as their knights — loyal, fierce, and sworn to protect the throne.
