Zhmer Smith

First King of the Royal Dragon Gang

FACTS
Status: One of the Four Pillars and founding members of the Royal Dragon Gang
Title Earned: The First King
Heritage: Half Filipino, Half American
Role: Supreme combat specialist and frontline enforcer
Combat Style: Mastery of firearms, weapons handling, and tactical marksmanship
Reputation: The deadliest shot among the Kings—precision incarnate

TRAITS
Extremely Dangerous – lethal efficiency in combat
Calm Under Fire – never misses when it matters
Protective Leader – shields his people without hesitation
Soft-Spoken Loyalty – kindness reserved only for those he loves

LORE
Zhmer Smith is the foundation upon which the Royal Dragon Gang was built
Born into an influential bloodline, he was raised not just with power—but with responsibility
While others learned to fight, Zhmer learned to dominate. Every weapon becomes an extension of his will, every battlefield his domain
As the First King, he stands at the front when blood is inevitable and at the back when protection is required. Enemies fear him for his aim.
Allies trust him for his heart. Cross him once—and you won’t get a second chance

Cleint Lee

Second King of the Royal Dragon Gang

FACTS
Status: One of the Four Pillars and founding members of the Royal Dragon Gang
Title Earned: The Second King
Heritage: Half Filipino, Half Korean
Specialty: High-speed racing, pursuit evasion, vehicular combat
Combat Style: Speed-based dominance paired with ruthless precision
Reputation: The untouchable racer—no one outruns Cleint Lee

TRAITS
Cold and Detached – emotions never interfere
Silent Operator – speaks only when necessary
Fearless Under Pressure – danger sharpens his instincts
Unshakeably Loyal – devotion proven through action

LORE
Cleint Lee is motion perfected. The streets raised him, engines shaped him, and danger refined him
From underground races to deadly chases, Cleint learned that speed is survival—and control is power
As the Second King, he ensures no pursuit succeeds and no escape fails
When Cleint moves, the city moves with him—or gets left behind in wreckage
He doesn’t chase glory. He outruns death

John Tedd Dela Serna

Fourth King of the Royal Dragon Gang

FACTS
Status: One of the Four Pillars and founding members of the Royal Dragon Gang
Title Earned: The Fourth King
Profession: Renowned vocalist of the Midnight Blue Band
Combat Style: Advanced hand-to-hand combat; fist-only fighting
Special Skill: Extreme close-range dominance
Reputation: A walking red flag—feared and desired in equal measure

TRAITS
Musically Gifted – commands crowds as easily as fear
Extremely Dangerous – especially in close combat
Arrogant and Reckless – unapologetically so
Fiercely Loyal – deeply protective of those he claims as his own

LORE
A star on stage and a nightmare in the streets, he thrives in contradiction. His fists end fights just as decisively as his voice steals hearts
Fame never softened him—it sharpened him. As the Fourth King, he embodies unpredictability
Underestimate him once, and you won’t survive long enough to regret it
Love him or fear him—either way, you’ll remember him
